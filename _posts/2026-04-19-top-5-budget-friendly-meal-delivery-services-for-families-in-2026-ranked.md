## The Ultimate Guide to Budget-Friendly Meal Delivery Services for Families

The family meal delivery space is buzzing with various options, each promising healthy, delicious, and convenient meals. But which ones are truly budget-friendly? After testing out several brands over the past year, I've narrowed it down to the top five services that keep both your stomach and wallet satisfied.

### 1. EveryPlate

#### Affordable Pricing

EveryPlate is known for being one of the most economical options available. I found their meals starting at just **$4.99 per serving**. With prices like this, it’s an undeniable contender for families on a budget. You can feed a family of four without spending your kid's college fund! 

#### Menu Selection

Their menu frequently changes, but you can expect a good mix of comfort foods like creamy pasta dishes and adventurous options like Asian stir-fries. During my first order, I tried their **Cheesy Beef and Mac**, which was a hit with my kids.

### 2. Home Chef

Home Chef stands out with its customizable meal plans, allowing you to swap proteins and add extra servings. They also offer a range of family-sized portions, perfect if you're feeding more than just a couple of mouths. I found their family meals to be hearty enough to feed leftovers for lunch the next day!

### 3. HelloFresh

#### Meal Options and Variety

What makes HelloFresh particularly appealing is their variety. With options like quick-prep meals, you can whip up something satisfying even on chaotic nights.

### 4. Blue Apron

## What Most Guides Get Wrong

When it comes to comparing meal delivery services, many guides often gloss over the following aspects:

1. **Hidden Fees**: If you don’t read the fine print, you may end up hit with shipping costs or charges for extra servings. For example, EveryPlate has a shipping fee that applies to smaller orders, while most others have free shipping above a certain threshold.

2. **Portion Sizes**: Many reviews focus on pricing without mentioning serving sizes. I’ve found that some brands offer smaller portions than advertised, potentially leaving you hungry or leading to food waste. For a family of four, I consistently had to order extra servings with Factor_.

3. **Meal Preparation Time**: While most services claim to have quick meal prep times, I often found that some meals took longer than advertised. For instance, my experience with HelloFresh's **15-minute meal** actually turned into a 30-minute endeavor.

The average cost ranges from **$4.99 to $11 per serving**, depending on the service. EveryPlate is usually the cheapest, while Factor_ leans on the higher end for pre-cooked nutritional meals.
