## How Does Tax Software Work?

Tax software automates the process of preparing your tax return. It guides you through various tax scenarios and deductions using a question-and-answer format. 

- **Ease of Use:** Most software simplifies the necessary steps, reducing the chances of mistakes.
- **Guided Assistance:** Often includes tips and explanations about tax laws changing annually.
- **e-Filing:** Allows you to submit your tax forms electronically to the IRS, expediting your refund process.

## How Much Does Tax Software Cost in 2026?

The cost of tax software varies widely depending on the features you need and the complexity of your tax situation. 

- **Basic Packages:** Generally range from $19.99 to $39.99, suitable for simple returns.
- **Standard Packages:** Typically range between $49.99 and $79.99, which cover itemizations and additional forms.
- **Premium Services:** Can cost upwards of $119.99 for self-employed individuals and complex returns.

In 2026, you can expect to pay about 10-15% more compared to previous years, given inflation and tech upgrades.
